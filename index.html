Lightweight Metals

Alloy Selection Science

Mechanical properties alone do not tell the full story — we consider printability, post-processing requirements, and final service environment for every recommendation.

  • ● AlSi10Mg elongation: 3.5% — stiff aerospace structures
  • ● 316L elongation: 50% — ductile energy applications
  • ● Ti6Al4V: density 4.43 g/cm³ — strength/weight champion
  • ● Inconel 718: operates -200°C to +700°C
  • ● CoCr modulus: 210 GPa — stiff medical grade
  • ● 17-4 PH hardness: equals forged H13 tool steel
